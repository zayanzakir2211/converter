About The Hydra Tools

The Hydra is a free, browser-based toolbox for encoding, decoding, hashing, and secure password generation. Everything runs client-side for speed and privacy.

Why We Built This

  • Provide fast, reliable converters for developers and students.
  • Keep data on-device for privacy-first usage.
  • Offer a clean, ad-light experience that supports itself via AdSense.
